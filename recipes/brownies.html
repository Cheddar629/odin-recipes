Brownies Recipe

Description:

Check out this new brownies recipe! It's both easy to do and cheap as well!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 talespoons water
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
