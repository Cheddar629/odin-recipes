Brownies Recipe

Description:

Check out this new brownies recipe! It's both easy to do and cheap as well!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 talespoons water
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 C. Grease an 8x8 inch square pan.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar, butter and water. Cook over medium heat until boiling. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted and smooth. Mix in the eggs and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and slat; stir into the chocolate mixture. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until brownies set up. Do not overbake! Cool in pan and cut into squares.
