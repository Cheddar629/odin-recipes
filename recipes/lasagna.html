Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Check out this lasagna recipe that is really easy to do! It's faster to do and less expensive than normal lasagnas!

Ingredients:

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzrella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint riccota cheese
