Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Check out this lasagna recipe that is really easy to do! It's faster to do and less expensive than normal lasagnas!

Ingredients:

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzrella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint riccota cheese

Directions:

  1. Bring a large pot of light salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain
  2. Preheat oven to 175 C. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in phaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and riccota; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
